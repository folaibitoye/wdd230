Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Ilesha Site

Sun Valley Chamber of Commerce

This name was selected because it represents the local business community in Sun Valley and emphasizes the role of the chamber in fostering economic growth.

Site Purpose

1. To provide resources and networking opportunities for local businesses.

2. To promote economic growth by showcasing business opportunities and events in Sun Valley.

Scenarios

• What local business events are happening this month?

• How can I become a member of the chamber?

• Where can I find economic data about Sun Valley for business expansion?
